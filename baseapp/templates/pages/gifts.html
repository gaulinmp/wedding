Gifts / Registry

We request the only presents be your presence. Coming to a wedding can be a costly endeavour, both in time and money, and we don't want to compound that with gifts on top of all the rest.

However, if you feel compelled to, we have a registry here.

The registry is through Zola. We have registered for group gifts because we mostly added big ticket items like airplane tickets for the honeymoon. Also as a note, when you contribute to a group gift, we (Lacey and Mac) see it, but your personal information is hidden from the public. So don't worry, your gifts are being received and loved.
